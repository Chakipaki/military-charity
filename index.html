ABOUT US

CHARITABLE ORGANIZATION "CHARITABLE FUND "VOLUNTEER MOVEMENT"" has been established in 2014 during Maidan Revolution to provide aid to those who were fighting for us. In 2022 during Russian-Ukrainian war the foundation has expanded its activities worldwide and providing humanitarian aid, means of protection and medicine to those in need on Ukrainian soil.

Our foundation is partner of UN and UNICEF.
